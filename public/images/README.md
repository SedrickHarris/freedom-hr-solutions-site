# Freedom HR Solutions — Public Image Assets

## Folder Structure

brand/      Logo assets and favicon variants
heroes/     Full-width hero background images
services/   Service page and hub card images
partners/   Partner and platform logo images
team/       Founder and team photos
blog/       Blog post featured images
og/         Open Graph social share images

## Naming Conventions

Heroes:   [page-slug]-hero.webp
Services: [service-slug].webp — must match route slug exactly
Partners: [platform-name]-logo.webp
Team:     [firstname-lastname].webp
Blog:     [post-slug]-featured.webp
OG:       [page-slug].webp or default.webp

## Format Rules

- .webp for all photos and illustrations
- Heroes: 1920x1080px minimum, under 200kb
- Service images: 800x500px (16:10 aspect ratio)
- Partner logos: from official brand press kits only
- Team photos: client-supplied only
- OG images: 1200x630px

## Hard Rules

No stock photography. No AI-generated images.
No competitor imagery. No screenshots as logos.
All images must be client-supplied or from
official brand assets.
Partner logos require client confirmation before use.

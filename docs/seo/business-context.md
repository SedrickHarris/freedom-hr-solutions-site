## What This Business Does
Freedom HR Solutions helps businesses manage HR compliance, payroll and benefits administration, ACA reporting, HR technology implementation, system audits, open enrollment setup, employee handbook creation, and ongoing HR technical support. Services are delivered through remote consulting and on-site support in the Las Vegas Valley.

## Primary Services
- HR MRI Risk Assessment
- HR compliance support
- ACA reporting consulting
- Payroll, benefits, and HR system management
- HR system setup and implementation
- HR technology and automation
- Open enrollment setup
- Reporting and data insights
- System audits and improvements
- Technical issue resolution
- Ongoing technical support
- Employee handbook creation and management
- Talent and workforce development

## Primary Audience
- Business owners managing HR without dedicated staff
- Small businesses without an internal HR department
- Growing businesses scaling HR operations
- HR teams needing additional capacity or specialized support
- Payroll teams managing system issues and multi-state payroll
- Benefits administrators managing open enrollment and ACA reporting
- Multi-state employers needing consistent compliance across jurisdictions

## Key Differentiators
- Platform-agnostic: works with ADP, isolved, BambooHR, HiBob, CustomHCM, and other HR/payroll platforms
- Las Vegas headquartered with nationwide remote delivery
- HR, payroll, benefits, and ACA reporting support under one firm
- Process-first approach: structured review before recommendations
- Founder-led by Soleil Kline with experience in HR technology, payroll, benefits, compliance, HR operations, and HCM systems
- Serves businesses without large internal HR teams
